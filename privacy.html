Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide via forms on our website (such as your name, email address, and phone number).

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used to:

  • Respond to your freight shipping inquiries;
  • Send informational SMS messages about your shipment (if SMS consent is given);
  • Improve the quality of our services.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your SMS consent or phone numbers with third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. SMS Messaging

If you check the SMS consent box, we will send informational SMS messages related to your shipment. You can opt out at any time by replying “STOP”.

5. Data Security

We use standard security practices to protect your personal data.

6. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this policy, email us at privacy@repofgeorgia.com.

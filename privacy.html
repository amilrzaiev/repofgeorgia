SMS Text Messaging Terms & Conditions – Rep of Georgia

By opting in to receive SMS (text) messages from Rep of Georgia, you agree to the following terms and conditions. These terms outline how we handle your data, the nature of our communications, and your rights as a subscriber.

1. Privacy Commitment

We take your privacy seriously. Any phone numbers collected for SMS consent are strictly confidential and will never be shared, sold, or disclosed to third parties for marketing purposes.

2. Types of SMS Communications

If you consent to receive SMS messages from Rep of Georgia, you may receive messages related to:

Appointment reminders or confirmations

Shipment updates or delivery information

Follow-up messages regarding transport services

Billing notifications or account-related inquiries

Note: We do not send promotional messages unless you have specifically opted in for such content.

3. Message Frequency

The number of messages you receive may vary depending on the nature of your interaction with Rep of Georgia.

Example: “You may receive up to 2 SMS messages per week related to your shipment status, appointments, or billing updates.”

4. Standard Messaging Fees

Standard message and data rates may apply, depending on your mobile carrier’s plan. Charges may vary for domestic or international messages.

5. How You Opt-In

You can opt-in to receive SMS communications from us by:

Submitting an online form with the SMS checkbox selected

Providing verbal consent during a conversation

Filling out a paper consent form

6. How You Opt-Out

You can opt-out of SMS messages at any time by replying:

STOP – To unsubscribe from all future text messages

You may also contact our support team directly to be removed from the messaging list.

7. Need Help?

If you are facing issues or have questions, simply reply with:

HELP – For SMS-related assistance

Or visit our for support

8. Additional Options

If you do not wish to receive text messages, you may simply leave the SMS consent box unchecked on our forms.

9. Standard Disclosures

Message and data rates may apply

You may opt-out at any time by replying STOP

For assistance, reply HELP
